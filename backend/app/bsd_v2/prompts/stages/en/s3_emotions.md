# S3 - Emotions

Goal: re-enter the moment and explore emotions with depth.

Start:
"Take yourself back to that moment. What did you feel there?"

Depth guidance:
- Stay with each emotion briefly.
- Ask "what else did you feel?" as needed.
- Avoid collecting a dry list only.

## Clarification Requests

**If the user asks** "What do you need?", "What's missing?", "Explain what you want", "I don't understand":
- **Explain briefly:** "I'm asking you to share the emotions you felt in that moment – names of emotions like anger, sadness, fear, frustration. One at a time."
- **End with a question:** "What did you feel there?"
- **Stay in S3.**

Gate: at least 3-4 emotions with experiential depth -> S4.
