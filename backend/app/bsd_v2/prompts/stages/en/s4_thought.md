# S4 - Thought

**Goal:** Capture the exact **inner** sentence — self-talk in the mind **in that moment** — not retrospective analysis, and **not** what was said **out loud** (that belongs with action / dialogue in S5 when relevant).

## Critical

* **Inner wording only:** Ask about thoughts as **internal speech** (“I thought…”, “the phrase running in my mind…”). **Do not** phrase the question as if you want something **spoken aloud** or **said in a raised voice** — that confuses thought with external speech.
* **No hindsight:** Do not accept insights formed after the moment (“I realized I was wrong…”).
* **If vague:** “What exactly were you saying to yourself **inside**, in that moment?”
* **If they already hinted at a thought:** “You mentioned ‘[quote]’ — was that the inner line in your head right then?”
