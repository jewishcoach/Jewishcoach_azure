# HeyGen walkthrough script — Jewish Coach AI (English)

**Audience:** New users  
**Tone:** Warm, clear, educational — not clinical  
**Approx. length:** ~3–4 minutes at moderate pace (~450 words)

Use scenes as separate HeyGen clips or one continuous take with B-roll on screen recordings.

---

## Scene 1 — Opening (0:00–0:35)

**[AVATAR / VOICEOVER]**  
Welcome to **Jewish Coach AI** — an app that brings structured Jewish coaching into a focused chat experience.  

The methodology behind the coach follows a classic coaching arc used in Jewish coaching training: you move from describing what is happening today, to clarifying what you want, then identifying the gap between the two, uncovering patterns and deeper assumptions, and finally choosing a meaningful next step with commitment.  

It is a reflective conversation — not therapy and not a substitute for professional mental-health care.

**[ON SCREEN]**  
App logo or landing; short title card: “Jewish Coach AI — How it works”

---

## Scene 2 — The chat workspace (0:35–1:15)

**[VOICEOVER]**  
When you open a session, you land in the **chat workspace**.  

You type or speak to the coach on one side; the coach responds on the other. The conversation is built so each reply nudges you along the coaching path — asking precise questions, reflecting what you said, and helping you go deeper step by step.

Sometimes you will see a **station checkpoint** card in the chat — a natural pause where you can breathe, review where you are, and choose to continue when you feel ready. You can collapse or dismiss it; your conversation stays saved.

**[ON SCREEN]**  
Screen recording: scroll chat; highlight message bubbles; briefly show checkpoint card expand/collapse if visible.

---

## Scene 3 — Coaching phases (“Vision ladder”) (1:15–2:30)

**[VOICEOVER]**  
Alongside the chat you will notice the **coaching phases** — sometimes shown as a compact ladder or strip on smaller screens.  

They mirror the methodology in plain language:

1. **Permission** — agreeing to begin the coaching focus.  
2. **Actual** — describing your topic, what happened, feelings, thoughts, and what you did — the “facts on the ground.”  
3. **Desired** — how you would want it to look and feel.  
4. **Gap** — naming the distance between actual and desired.  
5. **Pattern** — noticing repeating behaviours or beliefs.  
6. **Paradigm** and **Stance** — the deeper story you tell yourself and the position you take toward life or others.  
7. **Forces** — exploring inner tensions that pull you in different directions.  
8. **Choice**, **Vision**, and **Commitment** — articulating a clear decision and what you will actually do next.

You do not manage these stages manually; the AI guides you. The ladder simply helps you see **where you are** in the journey. Tapping a phase can scroll you back to relevant moments in the thread.

**[ON SCREEN]**  
Desktop and mobile: side ladder / strip; tap one phase → scroll to matching messages.

## Scene 4 — Insights in chat (2:30–2:55)

**[VOICEOVER]**  
During longer sessions, **insights** may appear in a side panel — short summaries or highlights distilled from the conversation. Think of them as signposts, not judgments: they help you notice themes while you keep talking with the coach.

**[ON SCREEN]**  
Open insights panel if available; show placeholder vs populated state.

---

## Scene 5 — Dashboard (2:55–3:45)

**[VOICEOVER]**  
Your **dashboard** is where the app steps back from a single chat and shows your overall journey.

- **Settings** — profile basics such as display name and preferences so the coach can address you appropriately.  
- **Summary** — high-level activity: conversations, messages, and how your sessions spread across coaching phases.  
- **Goals & Reminders** — keep coaching intentions and gentle reminders in one place.  
- **History** — browse past conversations and reopen them.  
- **Inner View** — a dedicated space for reflections and inner-work notes tied to your practice.

Together, the dashboard turns many single chats into one continuous coaching story.

**[ON SCREEN]**  
Walk through each tab; 3–5 seconds per tab.

---

## Scene 6 — Closing (3:45–4:00)

**[VOICEOVER]**  
That is Jewish Coach AI in a nutshell: a methodology-grounded coach in your pocket, clear phases you can follow visually, and a dashboard that supports reflection between sessions.  

Start a conversation, take your time with each stage, and use the dashboard whenever you want the bigger picture.

**[ON SCREEN]**  
CTA: “Open Jewish Coach AI” / Sign-in screen.
